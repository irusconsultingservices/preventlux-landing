About PreventLux

PreventLux is dedicated to transforming healthcare in Luxembourg by focusing on prevention rather than cure. We provide accessible resources, personalized risk assessments, and guidance to help individuals take control of their health before problems arise.

Healthcare today often emphasizes treatment after illness. PreventLux aims to shift this paradigm by promoting preventive care through education, regular health screenings, and lifestyle coaching. Our platform connects residents with expert advice, screening schedules, and tools to monitor key health indicators.

We collaborate with healthcare providers, insurance companies, and community organizations to ensure preventive care is integrated seamlessly into daily life in Luxembourg. Our vision is a healthier population with fewer chronic diseases and lower healthcare costs.
